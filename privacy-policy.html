Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026 | SmartRatesLab Data Protection & Compliance

Introduction

SmartRatesLab ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website, use our financial calculators, or interact with our services. We adhere to strict data minimization principles and comply with applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Zero-Knowledge Financial Calculations: All mortgage, investment, budget, and other financial calculations are performed locally in your browser. No loan balances, income data, or other sensitive inputs are ever transmitted to our servers.

Information We Collect

SmartRatesLab does not require user registration, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or financial account numbers. However, like most websites, we automatically collect certain non-identifying information via standard web technologies.

Log Files

SmartRatesLab follows standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they access our website. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information. We use this information to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement around the site, and gather demographic information for aggregate use.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many other websites, SmartRatesLab uses "cookies" to enhance user experience by storing preferences and tracking anonymized session data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We also use "web beacons" (clear GIFs, pixel tags) to monitor user engagement and the effectiveness of our content. Web beacons help us compile aggregated statistics about site usage and response rates. You may choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain interactive features of our calculators (e.g., saving temporary inputs).

  • Session Cookies: Temporary and expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until deleted or they expire, used for preference memory.
  • Web Beacons: Used in conjunction with cookies to understand user behavior patterns.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on SmartRatesLab. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. DART uses "non personally identifiable information" and does NOT track personal information about you, such as your name, email address, physical address, etc. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Alternatively, users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Our Advertising Partners

Some of the advertising partners on SmartRatesLab may use cookies and web beacons. Our advertising partners include, but are not limited to:

  • Google AdSense (DoubleClick)
  • Media.net
  • Amazon Associates (display advertising)

Each advertising partner has its own Privacy Policy. For your convenience, we provide links to their policies:

  • Google: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Media.net: https://www.media.net/privacy-policy
  • Amazon: https://developer.amazon.com/support/legal/tou

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to deliver advertisements and links that appear on SmartRatesLab directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons) may also be used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns or to personalize the advertising content you see. SmartRatesLab has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

User Choice & Opt-Out: You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings, or by managing preferences in programs such as Norton Internet Security. However, this may affect how you interact with our site and other websites.

GDPR Compliance (For European Economic Area Users)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have specific data protection rights under the GDPR. SmartRatesLab aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your personal data. Because we collect minimal non-identifiable log data only, the rights apply to any indirect identifiers we may process. These rights include:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Object to certain data processing activities.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another controller.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at supportentry1@gmail.com. We will respond within one month. Please note that we may ask you to verify your identity before responding to such requests.

CCPA Compliance (For California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California consumers have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold.
  • Delete personal information held by businesses.
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (SmartRatesLab does not sell personal information).
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights.

SmartRatesLab does not sell your personal information. In the preceding 12 months, we have not sold any personal information of consumers, including minors under 16 years of age. To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us with the subject line "CCPA Request" at supportentry1@gmail.com. We will verify your identity using the email address associated with your request.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

SmartRatesLab does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

Third-Party Links

Occasionally, we may include links to third-party products, services, or educational resources on our site. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

Data Security & Retention

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. Log files are retained for a maximum of 14 months for analytical purposes, after which they are automatically rotated or deleted. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, ISO 27001-certified servers, and all data transmissions between your browser and our website are encrypted via TLS (HTTPS).
