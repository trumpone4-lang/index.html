Contact SmartRatesLab

Direct communication for support, feedback, and partnership opportunities

We Value Your Input

SmartRatesLab is committed to delivering high-precision financial tools with uncompromising accuracy. Your feedback, bug reports, and feature suggestions are essential to our continuous improvement. Whether you have identified a discrepancy in a calculation, wish to propose a new financial model, or require technical support for one of our eight calculators, our team is ready to assist.

As a developer-first FinTech platform, we take every inquiry seriously. Each message is reviewed by our engineering and editorial teams to ensure that our calculators remain aligned with standard financial formulas, CFA guidelines, and real-world applicability. We encourage users to provide detailed information when reporting potential issues, including the calculator name, input values used, and expected versus observed outputs.

Primary Contact Channel

Email is the most efficient method to reach our support and operations team. We do not currently offer telephone support, as written correspondence allows us to document, track, and resolve technical or financial modeling inquiries with greater accuracy.

Response Time Commitment
Our standard response window is 24 to 48 hours (Monday through Friday, excluding US federal holidays). Inquiries submitted over weekends or holidays will be addressed on the next business day. For complex bug reports involving calculator logic, we may require additional time for verification and testing. You will receive an acknowledgment within 24 hours confirming receipt.

Types of Inquiries We Handle

Bug Reports & Technical Issues

If you encounter unexpected behavior in any calculator (e.g., Mortgage, Car Refinance, FIRE Simulator, Credit Card Points, Investment, Budget, Inflation, or College Savings), please include the following in your email:

  • Exact calculator name and URL.
  • All input values you entered (loan amount, interest rate, term, etc.).
  • The output you received versus the output you expected.
  • Browser name and version (e.g., Chrome 124, Safari 17).
  • Screenshots if applicable.

Detailed bug reports enable our engineering team to replicate conditions and deploy fixes rapidly. We are grateful to users who contribute to the reliability of SmartRatesLab.

Partnership & Integration Inquiries

SmartRatesLab welcomes collaboration opportunities with financial institutions, educational platforms, media outlets, and technology partners. If you are interested in embedding our calculators via iframe, API access (coming soon), co-branded content, or cross-promotion, please direct your inquiry with the subject line "PARTNERSHIP INQUIRY" to the email address above. All partnership discussions are confidential and reviewed by our business development team.

Feedback & Feature Requests

We actively monitor user suggestions for new calculators, additional parameters, or enhanced visualization features. While we cannot guarantee implementation of every request, each submission is cataloged in our product roadmap. Popular requests — such as Monte Carlo simulations, currency conversions, or tax-adjusted returns — are prioritized based on engineering feasibility and user value.

Media & Press Inquiries

Journalists, bloggers, and financial analysts seeking commentary on personal finance trends, calculator methodologies, or FinTech innovation may contact our communications desk. Please use "MEDIA INQUIRY" in the subject line and include your publication name, deadline, and specific questions. We respond to verified media requests within 12 hours during business days.

What We Do Not Support Via Email

SmartRatesLab does not provide personalized financial advice, investment recommendations, or loan approval services. Our team is not licensed as financial advisors, and we cannot review individual financial situations. For personal financial planning, please consult a certified financial planner (CFP) or licensed professional. Emails requesting personal financial advice will receive a standard response directing the user to our disclaimer and educational resources.

  • We do not offer account management or portfolio review services.
  • We do not sell financial products or receive commissions from lenders.
  • We do not provide tax preparation or legal counsel.

Data Privacy Assurance

Any information you provide via email (including your email address, name, or attached screenshots) will be used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and improving SmartRatesLab. We do not share email correspondence with third-party advertisers or marketing lists. For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy. We retain email logs for a maximum of 12 months for quality assurance and then delete them permanently.

Operational Hours & Holiday Schedule

Our team operates on Eastern Time (ET), USA. Standard monitoring hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM ET, Monday through Friday. SmartRatesLab observes the following US federal holidays, during which email responses may be delayed:

  • New Year's Day (January 1)
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day (third Monday in January)
  • Presidents' Day (third Monday in February)
  • Memorial Day (last Monday in May)
  • Juneteenth National Independence Day (June 19)
  • Independence Day (July 4)
  • Labor Day (first Monday in September)
  • Thanksgiving Day (fourth Thursday in November)
  • Christmas Day (December 25)

During major holiday closures, we post a notification banner on the website. Urgent bug reports related to critical calculation errors will be prioritized upon reopening.
